## Who We Are

**CycleChain** is a **professional technology studio** where art and engineering converge. Guided by our mantra **“Art of/and Code,”** we fuse creativity with performance and security.

## What We Build

* **Web & Frontend**: Immersive 3D/interactive experiences with ThreeJS & **GLSL**; enterprise-grade **design systems** with **SASS architecture** and modern CSS frameworks.

* **Backend & Data**: High-performance services in **Laravel/PHP**, **JavaScript**, **Python**, **Go**, **Rust**; resilient data layers across **SQL**, **NoSQL**, and **Redis**.

* **Blockchain Development**: Secure **Solidity** smart contracts with **Hardhat**, comprehensive testing, and professional deployments.

* **AI & ML**: Practical **LLM** integrations, **fine-tuning**, **prompt engineering**, and **custom datasets**—we implement value, not just models.

* **Mobile & Product**: End-to-end **UI/UX** and API design; example works include influencer platforms and museum kiosk interfaces.
